19th CGISACON 2024 Paper Presentation Guidelines

Detailed guidelines for paper abstract submission and presentation.

Guidelines for Paper Abstract Submission

Email for abstract submission: cgisacon2024academics@gmail.com

Word Limit: 250-300 words (excluding Title/References)

Title Page should include:

  • Title
  • Name of presenter
  • Qualification
  • Mobile No
  • Email
  • Institute
  • Status

Undertaking Page: Confirm the scientific work is original and should be signed by the author.

Abstract Structure:

  • Introduction
  • Methodology
  • Statistical Analysis
  • Results
  • Conclusion

References: Include up to 5 relevant latest references.

Eligibility and Submission

Presenter must be a Member of ISA.

Abstracts must be submitted online by August 30, 2024.

Review Process

Abstracts will be reviewed by the review committee for format, scientific content, and presentation (both speech and slides). The final decision will be at the discretion of the Abstract Review Committee. Presenters will be notified accordingly.

General Instructions

  • Presenter must be a member of ISA & must have registered for the conference.
  • Title should include the ISA logo and institutional logo (preferably at the top right corner).
  • Provide legends for tables and figures.
  • Use UK English uniformly (e.g., anaesthesia, haemoglobin).
  • Provide the full form of abbreviations at first use.
  • Perform a plagiarism check before submission.
  • Mention if the full paper is already published or submitted for publication.
